Services

Kaks is a reliable, trustworthy, and efficient contractor specialized in development and execution of projects (small or large scale) . We take the load off your shoulders by providing our specialized contracting services.

What We do

Construction

We offer our clients direct access to a team of highly qualified and experienced specialists offering consultancy services in preliminary work, building design, construction management as well as construction supervision and maintenance work.

Contractor

We excel in selling products and solutions – matching a client's needs to what the company has to offer. Also we offer professional advice, guidance, and actionable solutions to businesses experiencing issues they can't deal with in-house.

Consultant

Our team of experienced consultants offers a comprehensive range of services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you are a first-time homebuyer, a seasoned investor, or a property owner looking to sell.

Water Engineering

We provide technical solutions for treating primary sea water, brackish water, well water, surface water and waste water utilizing membrane separation technologies – Microfiltration, Ultrafiltration, Nano filtration, Reverse Osmosis – together with chemical conditioning

Automobile Sales

We sell cars as well as value-adding services such as maintenance plans and warranties to customers. We also assist potential customers by matching their desires, needs, and budget to the appropriate car.
